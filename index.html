ABOUT MOLLY

Molly was born on March 1st 2015. She was born and raised in Tehran, Iran. That technically makes her a Persian cat, but she is actually a Domestic Short Hair(DSH). She is a very special cat and a very smart girl.

She knows a bunch of tricks and will do them if presented with the right treats :)
The tricks include:

  • Come here
  • Sit
  • Turn around
  • High five
  • Give paw
  • Play dead

So yeah! She is a pretty smart cookie.
